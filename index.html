About Me:

I am Justin Rose AKA Guru Ligma, and I am a AI Artist, just starting out. Using a site called NightCafe, located here, I create art pieces with whatever comes to mind. If you like my art shown on the example section, consider checking out my other work on my social media pages that can be found Here, or even support me on Patreon!

Background:

I grew up in a loving military family, raised to follow the idea that "honesty is the best policy", with parents in the field of engineering and computers, i grew up fascinated with all thing electronic and self taught myself how to code. Also am now pursuing a software development degree and now starting with a social media profile around AI.
